Who am I?

Zhaojicun (Jerry Zhao) is a multimedia artist integrating photography with emerging digital technologies like interactive media. With a foundation in traditional fine arts like painting and sculpture, Jerry brings these classical influences into his creative process, layering together animations, graphic designs, and installations—projected and physical.


These explorations have landed Jerry’s three-dimensional pieces in online exhibitions by institutions like the Museum of Contemporary Digital Art and Niio, and his art has been exhibited internationally at the Chengdu Contemporary Image Museum.


Based in New York, Jerry is pursuing a double major and minor at New York University, majoring in Photography & Imaging at Tisch and Integrated Design & Media at Tandon as well as Business of Entertainment, Media and Technology at Stern. In addition to his studies, he works as a gallery and digital media assistant at Bruce Silverstein Gallery and as a freelance photographer, producing digital campaigns for select clients such as DIOR and pursuing content creation.
